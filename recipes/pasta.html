Pasta

Description

Tender, perfectly cooked pasta served with a rich tomato sauce, fresh herbs, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese. Simple, comforting, and timeless.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil salted water and cook the pasta until al dente and drain.
  2. Warm the tomato sauce in a pan with olive oil and garlic
  3. Toss the pasta with the sauce. Sprinkle Parmesan on top and serve.
