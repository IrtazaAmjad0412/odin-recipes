Pizza

Description

Classic pizza with a golden crust, topped with tangy tomato sauce and melted, gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough to desired size on a lightly floured surface.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (400°F) for 10–12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and crust is golden. Drizzle olive oil if desired and serve hot.
