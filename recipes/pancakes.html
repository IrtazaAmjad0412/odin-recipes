Pancakes

Description

Fluffy pancakes studded with rich chocolate chips, lightly golden and perfect with a drizzle of syrup.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Stir in milk and egg until smooth. Fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Heat a pan with a little butter. Pour batter to form pancakes and cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook until golden. Serve warm.
